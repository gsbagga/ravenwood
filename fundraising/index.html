The U.S. Flag Program


Show your PATRIOTISM, and support the Ravenwood High School Marching Band.

Sign up now to be included in your neighborhood US Flag display celebration. For $50 per year, the RHS Marching Band will display a beautiful 3'x5' American Flag in your front yard on the celebration days shown below.

We will set up flags early the morning of the event day, and then take them down that evening. Please tell all your neighbors to join in on this great patriotic event. The more families that participate, the more dramatic the effect will be on your street!!!

Christmas Tree Pickup


The Ravenwood High School Marching Band students will HAPPILY come by your house and pick up your Christmas tree from the curb.

The band would appreciate a small donation for this service of at least $10. Place your donation in an envelope and either staple it to the tree or tape it to your mailbox. Proceeds benefit band students to help offset the costs of Band fees, instruments, repairs, music, lessons, and much more!
